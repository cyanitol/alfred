  5. Use of Cookies and Logs

    Demisto uses internet cookies. A cookie is a small text file that contains no Personally Identifiable Information and that is placed in the memory of your browser. If the lifetime of the cookie extends beyond the time you spend on the website, the file is saved for future reference and is referred to as a “persistent cookie”. We use cookies to allow us to identify Users in recurring visits and track application settings. Demisto logs IP addresses and referrers to enable us to analyze usage trends and learn how to improve our service to Users. We dispose of our logs on a periodic basis. Most browsers are set out to accept cookies but you can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. As is true of most web sites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. We use this information, which does not identify individual users, to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user bases as a whole. We do not link this automatically collected data to Personally Identifiable Information.
