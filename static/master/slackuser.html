Secure Content for Everyone

DBOT protects Slack users by monitoring communications in the background and warning them about malicious and harmful content in real time. DBOT warnings are non-intrusive and are delivered within the Slack interface.

Security Analyst Collaboration

Whether you are in the middle of a forensic investigation or simply trying to understand whether you have encountered a security issue, DBOT saves time and effort by delivering threat feed reputation for IP, URL and Files from multiple sources right within Slack. Simply upload a suspicious file into one of your Slack channels, type a URL or IP address or even copy an MD5 hash into Slack and threat information will be readily available to you in a matter of seconds. Oh yes, and you can also share this information with your colleagues in Slack while you’re at it!
